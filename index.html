How I do work

I believe we all work better if we simplify so we need to focus on few items.
So I we start setting our attention to three pillars only:

  • Health: Physical, Mental and Emotional health.
  • Money: Understood as a compensation for a life of service and meaning.
  • Love: learn to forgive yourself and others, and live with passion and compassion.

The process to follow is simple, which does not mean easy, because it will require you to go through uncomfortable reflexion, take difficult decisions and hard work to create better habits.
In summary we will follow the steps below:

  1. Define clear goals in each of the three pillars mentioned before.
  2. Evaluate your current situation with respect the three pillars.
  3. Identify skills and habits you need to fill the gap between your current situation and the desired ones.
  4. Find courses, coaches, books and other material to close the gap as soon as possible.
  5. Choose what you need to let go in order to make room to the new trainings and habits.
  6. Create a learning plan... focusing on one item at a time
  7. Implement the learning plan... with a support team that helps you to commit.
  8. Practice, practice, practice until you master it... then
  9. Repeat the process!
